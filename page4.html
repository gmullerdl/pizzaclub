D I Y

January 01, 2020

Material:

3 filler flower sprigs

2 pieces of greenery

1 main flower

Cut and Arrange Your Florals

Remove any unnecessary leaves, and cut extra long stems down.

- F I R S T -

Attach Your Greenery

Take the sprigs of greenery you selected and wire one at a time along one half of the metal hoop. Wrap the wire along the length of each stems, making sure to lift leaves.

Place the Filler Flowers

Take the smaller blooms you selected and begin wiring them into the sparser spots of the wreath. Use the same method you did for the greenery, but avoid wrapping the entire flower.
